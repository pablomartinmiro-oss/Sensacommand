# Sensa Padel — Business Domain Knowledge

## The Club

Sensa Padel is a 6-court padel club in Nashville, Tennessee. It's a premium, community-driven facility targeting young professionals, corporate groups, and padel enthusiasts. The vibe is casual, social, and bilingual (English/Spanish).

## Membership Tiers

| Tier | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|---------|
| Non-Member | Pay per play (~$40/session) | Court rental only |
| Standard | $200/month | Discounted court rates, member events |
| Unlimited | $350/month | Unlimited court access, priority booking, all events |

Revenue target: $45,000/month across court rentals, memberships, lessons, pro shop, events.

## Lead Lifecycle

1. **New** — First contact (walk-in, referral, social media, website, PlayByPoint)
2. **Hot Lead** — Visited 2+ times in 30 days, engaged with messages
3. **Cold Lead** — Visited once, no return in 14+ days
4. **Converted** — Signed up for membership
5. **Churned** — Cancelled or inactive 30+ days

Key conversion moment: casual player visits 4+ times → prime for membership pitch.

## Key Metrics Pablo Cares About

- **Daily/MTD revenue** vs $45k target (pace tracking)
- **MRR** (monthly recurring revenue from memberships)
- **Active member count** and churn risk
- **Court utilization** (target: 60%+ during peak, 30%+ off-peak)
- **New player acquisition** per week
- **Hot lead count** and conversion rate
- **Goal completion** across the team

## Communication Style

- **WhatsApp-first** — primary channel for player outreach
- **Casual and warm** — first names, emojis okay, not corporate
- **Bilingual** — some players prefer Spanish
- **Personal** — reference their play history, last visit, etc.
- **Action-oriented** — always include a next step or CTA

## Court Operations

- 6 courts total
- Peak hours: 5pm - 9pm weekdays, 9am - 2pm weekends
- Off-peak: 6am - 4pm weekdays
- Court types: all standard padel courts
